About Hotel

The 3-star Ruru Tower Aparthotel Manama is a great place to stay in Al Juffair, which is a great choice for travellers interested in Nightlife, Entertainment and Food, at a 2.1 km distance from Dolphin Resort Bahrain. Offering views of the city, the aparthotel is placed quite a walk from Juffair Square and boasts an outdoor pool.

The property is placed about 0.5 km from Bahrain airport and 10 minutes by car from such cultural venues as Beit Al Quran. The Ruru Tower Aparthotel Manama is also 4 km from such natural sights as the amusement park "Water Garden". You may prefer to explore the area's attractions, as the accommodation is fringed by a cinema, cafés and a coffee shop. Ruru Tower Aparthotel is set within convenient reach of Lulu Hypermarket.

There are 51 rooms comprising a mini-kitchen and a dining area. They come with free wireless internet and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, as well as such comforts as a trouser press and air-conditioning. The nice touches include a fireplace together with a sofa and a writing desk. Offering amenities such as free toiletries and towels, the bathrooms also feature a roll in shower. In addition, guests are welcome to use a kitchenette with a microwave, an electric kettle and an oven in a deluxe apartment.

Sample Turkish specialities at German Doner Kebab located nearly a 5-minute walk from this Manama hotel.
